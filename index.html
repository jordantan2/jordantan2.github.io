Jordan Tan

“First, solve the problem. Then, write the code.” – John Johnson

Jordan is a 21 year old student who grew up in the small coastal town of Margaret River, where her environment and community provided a positive and balanced lifestyle for her to explore her interests and hobbies.

She has lived independently since age 18 and now has a lifestyle near the ocean and in her second year of studies in Computer Science at Curtin University. She found her love for programming through her affinity to and enjoyment of mathematics growing up.

She is the youngest to receive a scholarship for the She-codes plus program which she completed in 2022.

Jordan is a motivated learner and is excited at the prospects of where her tech education will lead her in the near future. She hopes her work will continue to challenge her every day, and to balance her ambitions in the tech field with her love of food, music, fitness and the great outdoors.
