Jordan Tan

“First, solve the problem. Then, write the code.” – John Johnson

Jordan is a bright 21 year old who grew up in small town Margaret River. Living amoungst such serene surroundings allowed a positive and balanced lifestyle for her to explore her interests and hobbies. She now lives independently near the ocean in the northern suburbs of Perth and is in her second year of studies in Computer Science at Curtin University. She found her love for programming through her skill and enjoyment in mathematics growing up. She has worked in hospitality since the young age of 15, being a quick learner she was able to gain many great skills in the industry. Her strong work ethic, calm nature and attention to detail allowed her to work in high class restaurants and be promoted to supervisory roles. She hopes to carry her learnings with her into her next journey, working in her desired field. Her future in tech as a young and ambitious female is destined for success.
